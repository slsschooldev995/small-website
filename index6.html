Pancake Recipe

Ingredients for Pancakes

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 2 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon of melted butter

    • Steps to Make Pancakes

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in the milk,egg and melted egg.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour 1/2 cup of butter for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles from on the surface.

Glossary of cooking terms

saute
Cook quickly in a small amount of oil over high heat.
Bake
Cook food in an oven using dry heat.
Simmer
Cook gently just below boiling point.
